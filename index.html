about me

hello, my name is aisha kazembe. it is great to see you here. i am an engineer and artist. i graduated from the university of southern california with a b.s. in environmental engineering, class of 2022. i am focused on sustainability, with a particular interest in sustainability in the built environment. i enjoy working with water resources and sustainable building design, and have practical experience with environmental health, safety, and governance data transformation and management. in art, i am focused on built and natural environments, people, and how they interact with each other.
these interests amalgamate as i pursue a career in collaborative efforts to create a built environment that interacts harmonically with humans, the natural environment, and the eye.
